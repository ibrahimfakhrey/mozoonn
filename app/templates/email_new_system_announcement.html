New Dismissal Duty Attendance System

Dear {{ teacher_name }},

We are pleased to announce the implementation of a new Dismissal Duty Attendance System.

This system will help us track attendance during dismissal duties and ensure the safety of our students.

Late Arrival Policy
Occurrence Consequence
1st Late Warning Email
2nd Late 1/4 Day Salary Deduction
3rd Late 1/2 Day Salary Deduction (Counter Resets)
Absence Policy
Occurrence Consequence
1st Absence Warning Email
2nd Absence 1/2 Day Salary Deduction
3rd Absence 1 Full Day Salary Deduction (Counter Resets)
Important: After the 3rd occurrence (for both late arrivals and absences), the counter will reset and the cycle begins again. Please ensure punctual attendance at all assigned dismissal duties.

You will receive email notifications automatically when you are marked as late or absent from your assigned duties.

If you have any questions or concerns about this new system, please contact the administration.

Thank you for your cooperation and commitment to student safety.
